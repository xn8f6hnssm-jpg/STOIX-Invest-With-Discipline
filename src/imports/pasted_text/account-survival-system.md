**Prop Firm Success Improvements – Risk Intelligence & Behavior Protection Upgrade**

The Account Protection Center is a strong foundation but should feel more like an intelligent protection system rather than just a risk calculator. We need to add behavioral insights, clearer risk status, and AI recommendations.

Goal:
Make this feel like an **Account Survival System**, not just a dashboard.

---

**1. Add Overall Account Risk Status**

Add a section at the top:

**Account Risk Status**

Display a simple status badge:

Low Risk (Green)
Moderate Risk (Yellow)
Elevated Risk (Orange)
High Risk (Red)

Example display:

Account Risk Status:
🟢 LOW RISK

Based on:

Daily loss usage
Trade risk sizing
Behavior patterns

Example logic:

0–40% loss used → Low Risk

40–65% → Moderate Risk

65–85% → Elevated Risk

85–100% → High Risk

Goal:
Give traders an instant understanding of account safety.

---

**2. Add Behavior Risk Monitor**

Add new section:

**Behavior Risk Monitor**

This should analyze trading behavior patterns.

Examples:

No warnings:

"Your trading behavior is stable today."

After 2 losses:

"Loss streak detected. Revenge trading risk increases after multiple losses."

After position increase:

"Position size is larger than your average risk."

After excessive trades:

"Trade frequency higher than your normal behavior."

Goal:
Connect trader psychology to account protection.

---

**3. Add AI Risk Recommendations**

Under Plan Next Trade Risk add:

**AI Recommendation**

Examples:

"Based on remaining loss, consider risking no more than $300 per trade."

"Reducing size by 25% would allow approximately 3 more safe trades."

"At current risk, 2 losses would place account in danger zone."

Goal:
Make AI feel like a trading coach instead of just showing numbers.

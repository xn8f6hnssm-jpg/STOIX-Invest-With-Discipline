**AI Analysis Correction – Strategy Pattern Detection (Not Just Statistics)**

The current AI Analysis is showing basic statistics only (winrate, trade count, etc.). This is NOT the intended functionality.

AI should function as a **Strategy Pattern Detection Engine**, not just a statistics dashboard.

The purpose is to identify what combinations of factors actually make the trader profitable.

---

**Required Change**

AI must generate **written insights** based on pattern comparisons.

Do NOT only show metrics.

AI must detect:

Best performing trade conditions

Worst performing trade conditions

Behavior patterns affecting results

Strategy combinations producing highest winrate

Strategy combinations producing lowest winrate

---

**AI Must Compare Trade Variables**

AI should compare combinations of:

Strategy tag

Confluences

Risk Reward ratios

Time of day

Rule adherence

Emotional tags

Checklist completion

Trade direction

Then determine:

Which combinations produce the highest winrate.

Which produce the most profit.

Which produce the most losses.

---

**Required AI Output Sections**

Instead of only showing Overall Performance, add:

**Best Performing Conditions**

Example:

Best Setup Detected:

Liquidity Sweep + MSS + RR above 1:2

Winrate:
68%

Trades analyzed:
22

AI Insight:

"You win most when trading liquidity sweeps with confirmation and proper RR. Consider prioritizing this setup."

---

**Worst Performing Conditions**

Example:

Worst Pattern:

RR under 1.5

Trading after 1PM

No confirmation entry

Winrate:
31%

AI Insight:

"Trades taken with low RR and no confirmation perform significantly worse. Consider avoiding these conditions."

---

**Behavior Insight Section**

Example:

Checklist followed:

Winrate:
63%

Checklist ignored:

Winrate:
39%

AI Insight:

"You perform significantly better when following your checklist. Rule discipline is directly improving results."

---

**Strategy Recommendation Section**

AI must provide improvement suggestions.

Example:

"Your highest profit trades occur when RR is above 1:2. Consider avoiding trades below this level."

Example:

"Morning trades outperform afternoon trades. Consider focusing on your strongest time window."

---

**Important Instruction**

AI should always answer:

What works best?

What performs worst?

What behavior improves results?

What behavior hurts results?

Do NOT just show statistics.

AI must interpret the data.

---

**Design Goal**

AI should feel like:

A trading performance coach.

Not:

A numbers dashboard.

The trader should feel like AI is helping them refine their edge, not just reporting results.

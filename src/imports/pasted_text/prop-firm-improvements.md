**Prop Firm Success Improvements – Make Account Protection More Actionable**

The Prop Firm Success tab is a good foundation but currently feels like a statistics display. We need to make it more actionable and behavior-focused so traders feel it actively protects their account.

Focus on decision assistance, not just numbers.

---

**1. Add "Today's Trading Guidance" Section (Very Important)**

Add a new section at the top:

**Today's Trading Guidance**

AI should generate a simple daily recommendation based on current risk usage.

Examples:

If low loss used:

"You're within safe limits. Focus on high-quality setups only."

If 50% loss used:

"You've used half your daily loss. Consider reducing size."

If 70%+ used:

"High risk of rule violation. Trade smaller or stop today."

If 85%+ used:

"Account protection recommended. Consider stopping today."

Goal:
Give behavioral guidance instead of just data.

Prop Firm Success should feel like:

An account survival system.

Not a rule tracker.

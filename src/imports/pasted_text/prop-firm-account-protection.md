**Prop Firm Success Tab Redesign – Account Protection & Survival Tools**

The Prop Firm Success tab should not feel like a statistics page. It should function as an **Account Protection Center** that helps traders avoid failing prop firm rules and make safer decisions.

Primary goal:

Help traders answer:
**"Can I safely take my next trade?"**

Focus on:
Protection
Risk awareness
Decision assistance

---

**Rename Internal Purpose**

Prop Firm Success should function as:

**Account Protection Center**

Not just account rules display.

---

**1. Today's Limits Section (Top Priority)**

Add a top section called:

**Today's Limits**

Display:

Daily Loss Limit

Loss Used Today

Remaining Allowed Loss

Example:

Daily Limit:
$2,500

Used:
$1,800

Remaining:
$700

Add color system:

Green → Safe

Yellow → Caution

Red → Near violation

Add status indicator:

SAFE TO TRADE

TRADE CAREFULLY

STOP TRADING

Goal:
Give traders immediate clarity on risk status.

---

**2. Trade Safety Indicator**

Allow system to estimate if next trade is safe.

Based on:

Remaining daily loss

User's typical risk per trade

Show:

**Trade Safety Status**

Example:

Remaining allowed loss:
$700

Recommended max risk next trade:
$350

Message example:

"Risk above this increases rule violation probability."

Goal:
Help traders size properly.

---

**3. Risk Calculator Tool**

Add simple calculator:

Fields:

Instrument (example: NQ, ES, etc)

Contracts

Stop size

App calculates:

Total risk

Compare against remaining allowed loss.

Example output:

Trade Risk:
$900

Remaining allowed:
$700

Show warning:

**Trade exceeds allowed risk**

Suggestion:

Reduce contracts

Reduce stop

Goal:
Make tab actionable.

---

**4. Challenge Progress Tracker**

Add section:

**Challenge Progress**

Show:

Profit target

Current profit

Remaining target

Example:

Profit Target:
$8,000

Current:
$5,200

Remaining:
$2,800

Optional:

Estimated pace example:

"At $300/day average:
~10 trading days remaining."

Goal:
Motivation and progress tracking.

---

**5. Protection Alerts Section**

Add behavioral alerts:

Examples:

Used 70% of daily loss

Multiple losses detected

Rule violation risk increasing

Example warning:

"You have used 78% of your daily loss limit."

Suggestion:

"Consider stopping trading today."

Goal:
Behavior interruption before failure.

---

**6. Daily Stop Recommendation**

When trader approaches limits:

Show:

**Protection Recommendation**

Example:

"One more average loss could violate rules."

Suggestion:

Stop trading

Reduce size

Switch to simulation

Goal:
Help traders protect accounts.

---

**7. Design Layout Structure**

Prop Firm Success tab should contain:

Today's Limits (top)

Trade Safety Indicator

Risk Calculator

Challenge Progress

Protection Alerts

Keep layout simple and serious.

This should feel like a control center.

---

**Design Goal**

This tab should feel like:

"A system designed to help you NOT blow your funded account."

Not:

"Extra statistics."

It should actively help traders:

Avoid violations

Manage risk

Make safer decisions

Protect funded accounts

This should become one of the main Premium value features.
